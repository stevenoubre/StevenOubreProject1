Experience

As I began my career in college athletics as a graduate assistant, I was introduced to UI and UX design in classes I was talking. I loved how development met design. Not just the interface, but the importanc of good user flow to create a successful project.

I knew I was going to need to learn a little bit of code to communicate with developers. But then I figured why not learn code and have complete control over the whole process? It certainly helps to have a team with people that can do everything, but I want to know everything that goes into creating digital products.

I'm excited to get started with my first project with the TreeHouse Tech Degree program.
